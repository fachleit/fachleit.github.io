research interests

applications: fluid dynamics (water waves), anomalous diffusion, structural dynamics, optimal (random) search strategies, structure preserving numerical methods
models: (viscous) hyperbolic conservation laws, reaction-diffusion equations, kinetic equations (of Fokker--Planck and Boltzmann type) in statistical physics
concepts: existence, regularity and (meta)stability of solutions, asymptotic behavior of solutions, hypocoercivity (with quantitative decay rates)
methods: dynamical system theory, semigroup theory, spectral analysis, entropy/energy method
